Apollo, Dreams, and Poetry

Blessed Be, Almighty Me!

Welcome to Apollo, Dreams, and Poetry!
This is where the lost poets,
Artists,
And musicians
Belong.
The Olympian Champions!


But careful not to outshine,
The Golden God,
Whom is no other than I,
Phoebus Apollo,
The Almighty,
The Sun,
The Creative,
The God of all Arts,
Medicine,
Archery,
Prophecies,
And of course,
Beauty,
But not to be confused with the
Almighty Goddess Aphrodite,
For even I shall not
Incur Her wrath.

May you never dare
To challenge me.
Lest you be flayed,
As the Great Satyre did.

Nonethless,
I welcome thee
To the Publication of
Apollo, Dreams, and Poetry!
It is an honour
For you,
And not Me,
to be here.

By: Cyrl's Corner

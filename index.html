Apollo Dreams and Poetry

Welcome to Apollo, Dreams, and Poetry!

This is where the lost poets, artists, and musicians land.
The Olympian Champions!
But careful not to outshine,
The Golden God,
Whom is no other than I,
Phoebus Apollo,
The Almighty,
The Sun,
The Creative, The God of all Arts, Medicine, Archery, Prophecies, And of course, Beauty, But not to be confused with the Almighty Goddess Aphrodite, For even I shall not Incur her wrath.
May you never dare to challenge me. Lest you be flayed, As the Great Satyre did. Nonethless, I welcome thee To the Publication of Apollo, Dreams, and Poetry! It is an honour For you, And not Me, to be here. By: Cyrl's Corner
